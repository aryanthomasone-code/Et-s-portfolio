ABOUT

Hi, I’m Aryan Thomas, a third-year design student at the Dubai Institute of Design and Innovation. My work sits at the intersection of function-first thinking and a curiosity for artistic media.


I design across product, branding, and UI/UX, balancing experimentation with structure. I’m drawn to ideas that start off rough and unfiltered, then gradually evolve into something purposeful and functional—somewhere between a kid imagining what could exist and a part-time engineer figuring out how to make it real.


This portfolio is a selection of those explorations—curated multidisciplinary work that reflects that process.
